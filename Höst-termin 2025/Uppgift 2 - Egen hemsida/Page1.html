Welcome to our Amazing Huddinge Kommuns Nature

Smell the nature

Huddinge, just south of Stockholm, is known for its beautiful natural landscapes. The area features dense forests, clear lakes like Orlången and Gömmaren, and protected nature reserves.

These spots offer great opportunities for hiking, biking, swimming, and birdwatching. The local wildlife includes deer, birds, and fish, while the forests bloom with wildflowers and berries.

Huddinge is a peaceful escape for anyone who loves outdoor activities year-round.

1. Flottsbro Recreation Area

Flottsbro Recreation Area, located 20 km south of Stockholm, offers skiing, snowboarding, and sledding in winter, and hiking, biking, and swimming in summer.

The area features a beginner-friendly ski slope, scenic trails, and Lake Flottsbro for water activities.

It also has a café, picnic areas, and a playground, making it a popular spot for outdoor fun year-round.

2. Orlången Nature Reserve

Orlången Nature Reserve, established in 2010, is located south of Stockholm in Huddinge.

It covers 1,732 hectares with open fields, rocky hills, and a typical Södertörn landscape.

The area hosts rare plants, birds like tawny owls, and bats. It has historical sites and several hiking trails, including a wheelchair-accessible path.

The reserve includes three lakes: Orlången, Ågestasjön, and Mörtsjön.

3. Gömmarens Nature Reserve

Gömmarens Nature Reserve in Sweden is known for its beautiful old-growth forests and diverse wetlands,

providing a rich habitat for many rare plants and animals.
